What this means

We have passed the information the caller provided to all relevant government and local authority departments. They will suspend any services and benefits Jane Smith received.

For example, if they:

  • had a State Pension, this will be suspended
  • got benefits, like Employment and Support Allowance, this will be suspended
  • used a Blue Badge, this will be suspended

The caller will get a letter confirming this notification within 7 working days.

What happens next?

When the death is registered with a registrar, the death will be confirmed.

This confirmation will then be passed on to relevant government and local authority departments. They will cancel any services and benefits that Jane Smith was entitled to.

The caller will get a letter confirming which services and benefits have been cancelled within the next 4 weeks.
