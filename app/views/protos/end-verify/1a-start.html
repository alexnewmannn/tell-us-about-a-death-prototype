Report a death

When someone dies, you can let most government organisations know in one go.

As soon as you feel ready, you'll need to give some details about the person who died. You'll only need to do this once.

The information you give will be used to:

  • stop any benefits or services - for example, their State Pension
  • protect their identity - for example, by cancelling their passport
  • prevent overpayments from any benefits they may have received

Before you start

You'll need your National Insurance number, as well as:

  • the name of the person who died
  • their National Insurance number
  • their date of death
  • their date of birth
  • their most recent postcode
Start now

Staying updated

After you have given details about the death, you can choose to get updates on what's happening.

You’ll need to prove your identity before you can get updates. There’s information about how to do this at the end of this service.
