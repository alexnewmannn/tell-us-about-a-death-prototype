Tell us about a death

When someone dies, any allowances or benefits they received will need to be cancelled.

As soon as you can, you'll need to provide some details about the person who has died. This may help to prevent overpayment of any benefits they received, and fraudulent use of their identity.

Information you will need to provide

  • The name and National Insurance number of the person who died

Information you may need to provide

You may need to provide your name and National Insurance number.
