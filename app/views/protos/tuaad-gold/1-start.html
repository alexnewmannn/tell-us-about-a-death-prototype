Tell us about a death

When someone dies, Government and Local Government departments need to be informed so that any services they received can be cancelled.

How it works

As soon as you can, you'll need to provide some details about the person who has died. This may help to prevent overpayment of any benefits they received, and fraudulent use of their identity.

You'll be shown a list of the departments we'll contact on your behalf, and you'll be sent a confirmation notice when each department has completed their process.

The following process takes around ten minutes to complete.

Information you will need to provide about the person who has died

  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Postcode
  • National Insurance Number

Information you will need to provide about yourself

  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • National Insurance Number
  • Contact details
